Assignment AI Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 March 2026

Assignment AI helps you generate handwritten-style assignments from your own handwriting samples and background pages. This policy explains how we handle your data.

What We Collect

  • Handwriting and background images you choose to upload or select from your device.
  • Text you type to generate assignment pages.
  • Basic app state stored locally on your device so the app can work smoothly.

How We Use Your Data

  • To generate handwriting-style output, match fonts, and render assignment pages.
  • To download the handwriting font pack when needed.
  • To help troubleshoot if you contact support.

Where Processing Happens

  • Most processing happens locally on your device.
  • Some features may send images to a backend service you configure in the app for font generation or style extraction. Those images are used only to produce your result.

Sharing and Selling

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not share your data with third parties except service providers required to run backend features.

Data Retention

  • Your content stays on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • Backend services may temporarily store files to complete processing. We do not intend to keep them longer than necessary to provide the result.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us and we will delete it.

Your Choices

  • You can choose not to upload handwriting and still use basic features.
  • You can remove content by deleting files from your device or uninstalling the app.
