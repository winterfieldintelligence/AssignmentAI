Assignment AI Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 March 2026

Overview

  • Assignment AI converts your content into a handwriting-style page.
  • You control what content is processed.
  • This policy explains how data is handled.

Data We Collect

  • Images you upload or capture (handwriting samples, page backgrounds, optional note photos).
  • Text you type or text extracted on device.
  • App settings stored locally on your device.

How We Use Data

  • Render handwriting-style pages.
  • Extract text from images you choose.
  • Match or generate handwriting-style fonts (when enabled).
  • Save generated pages to your device when you choose.

Where Processing Happens

  • Processing is done on your device by default.
  • If you enable optional backend services (font generation or font library download), selected images may be sent to that server only for processing.

Sharing And Selling

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not share data with third parties, except the optional backend you enable.

Data Retention

  • Content stays on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • Images saved to your gallery stay until you delete them.
  • Recent history and locally saved previews are removed when you delete your account.
  • Optional backend servers may keep files temporarily only to complete processing.

Permissions

  • Camera: capture handwriting, backgrounds, or note photos.
  • Photos and media: select images from your device.
  • Internet: optional font library download or optional backend processing.

Children Privacy

  • This app is not directed to children under 13.

Your Choices

  • You can use the app without uploading images by typing your content.
  • You can delete app data at any time from your device settings.
