Account Deletion Policy

Last updated: 18 March 2026

This page explains how account deletion works in the Assignment AI Android app.

1. How to delete your account

  1. Open the app and log in.
  2. Open the menu using the menu icon.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

If you cannot access the app, request deletion assistance at eknathyuvrajpatil@gmail.com or assiginmentaiapp@gmail.com.

2. What is deleted

When deletion is confirmed, account-related data stored by the app on your device is removed, including:

  • Current login/session data.
  • Account-related preferences stored in app storage.

3. What is not deleted automatically

  • Images or files you manually saved to device storage.

These files are saved as normal device files. You can remove them anytime using your device file manager.

4. Data retention and timing

  • Account data used for login is stored locally on your device (not on developer-controlled account servers).
  • Deletion in the app is applied immediately after confirmation.
  • No personal account login data is retained by the developer after deletion.

5. What happens after deletion

  • You are logged out of the app.
  • You are returned to the Login/Signup screen.
  • You can create a new account later in the app if you choose.
