Account Deletion Policy

Last updated: 19 March 2026

Overview

  • This page explains how account deletion works in Assignment AI.

Delete Your Account In App

  • Open the app and log in.
  • Open the menu (menu icon).
  • Tap Delete Account.
  • Confirm the request.

If You Cannot Access The App

  • Request deletion by email at eknathyuvrajpatil@gmail.com or assignmentaiapp@gmail.com.

What Is Deleted

  • Current login and session data stored on your device.
  • Account-related preferences stored in app storage.
  • Recent history and locally saved assignment previews.

What Is Not Deleted Automatically

  • Images or files you manually saved to device storage.
  • You can remove those anytime using your device file manager.

Retention And Timing

  • Login data is stored locally on your device, not on developer-controlled servers.
  • Deletion is applied immediately after confirmation in the app.
  • No personal account login data is retained by the developer after deletion.

After Deletion

  • You are logged out.
  • You are returned to the Login or Signup screen.
  • You can create a new account later if you choose.
